Calceus – Sneaker E-Commerce Platform

Calceus is a modern sneaker-focused e-commerce platform designed to deliver a seamless shopping experience with a strong emphasis on UI/UX, performance, and scalability. It enables users to explore, filter, and purchase sneakers efficiently while providing a robust backend for managing products and users.

🚀 Features
🛍️ Product Browsing & Search
Explore a wide range of sneakers
Advanced filtering (price, category, brand)
🛒 Cart & Checkout
Add/remove items from cart
Smooth checkout flow
📦 Order Management
Track orders
View purchase history
⚡ Responsive Design
Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop
